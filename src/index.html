About me

For as long as I can remember, I’ve loved solving problems. After taking computer science classes at a local community college, I began developing my analytical skills in a data analysis role within the imaging department at a pre-press company, followed by a start-up ISP. There I designed 3 basic websites using the platform Dreamweaver. Since then I’ve worked for Fortune 500 companies, and learned to adapt quickly to different work environments. Soon I realized I wanted to learn how to do more with data, which meant learning how to code and becoming a software developer.

I started to teach myself programming concepts online and then graduated a .NET programming bootcamp at Tech Elevator.

I get most excited about working at an innovative organization with a strong emphasis on collaboration, and craftsmanship. I love solving problems for a living!
